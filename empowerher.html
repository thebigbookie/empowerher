EmpowerHer Collective

"For Her From Us"

Our mission is to equip educationally wishful women and girls with the tools, skills
and support systems desired to achive economic support needed for them to reach the future they see so far off reach.



We Focus On:

    Education & Digital Access

    Scholarships, tech skills training, lapyop and internet access.

    Health & Safety

    Access to mental health counseling, reproductive health services, domestic violence and abuse survivor support.

    Leadership & Advocacy

    We provide programs to encourege women in political and comunity envolvement, public speaking and confidence building workshops and spaces and platforms for advocacy and story telling.

    Economic Empowerment

    Job readiness training, enterpreneurship support and financial literacy workshops.
